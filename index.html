How it works

The prediction lifecycle — lock to proof.

1
Prediction submitted & lockedAnalyst submits their pick via your platform. Verity Stack seals the prediction — timestamped and cryptographically committed — up to 60 seconds before event start. No backdating possible at any layer.
2
Commitment written to Verity LayerA unique fingerprint is written to the Verity Layer — our dedicated verification ledger on HGTP. Full prediction content stored on decentralized storage. The ledger record is permanent and publicly queryable.
3
Automatic resolution via dual oracleWithin seconds of the final whistle, two independent data providers confirm the outcome. Consensus via proof-of-verification. Automatic — no manual review, no disputes.
4
Proof Card issuedA verified Proof Card is generated — branded to your platform, shareable, and independently verifiable via QR code against HGTP. Correct calls become distributable acquisition assets.
